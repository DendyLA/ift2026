{% blocktrans %} The International Forum on Attracting Investment into the Private Sector, ‘Investing in the Future of Turkmenistan’ (IFT 2026), will take place on 18 March 2026 in Ashgabat, at the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Turkmenistan. The Forum is organised by the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Turkmenistan and the Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs of Turkmenistan, in partnership with TMT Consulting Group. IFT 2026 is designed to serve as an international platform for the comprehensive presentation of Turkmenistan’s economic and investment potential, the attraction of investment into the country’s private sector, and the expansion of mutually beneficial business ties between national and international partners. The Forum will be held in parallel with the International Exhibition of the Private Sector of Turkmenistan, enabling delegates to efficiently combine participation in two complementary events. In addition to the traditional B2B and B2G formats, opportunities for B2C engagement will also be created. {% endblocktrans %}
